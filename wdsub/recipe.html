Iaryna's Half-Brownie-Half-Cookie Recipe

Make the best "brookies"in town!

  1. Gather Your Ingredients

    • 4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 2 cups white sugar
    • 1 cup brown sugar
    • 2 tsp baking soda
    • 2 eggs
    • 1 and 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
    • 1 cup cocoa powder
    • 1 and 1/2 cup chocolate chips (optional)
    • 1 cup softened butter
    • Pinch of salt

  2. Prepare Your Kitchen

    • Preheat your oven to 375 degrees F
    • Gather two large bowls
    • Gather two medium bowls
    • Cover 2-inch-deep brownie pan with baking spray or a baking sheet
    • Place butter in room-temperature area to soften

  3. Combine the Wet Ingredients

    1. Cookie Mix

      1. In a large bowl, whisk 1 cup brown sugar, 1 cup white sugar, 1/2 cup softened butter, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, 1/2 cup softened butter
      2. Beat in 1 egg until smooth
      3. Set aside

    2. Brownie Mix

      1. In another large bowl, whisk 1 cup white sugar, 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
      2. Beat in 1 egg until smooth
      3. Set aside

  4. Combine the Dry Ingredients

    1. Cookie Mix

      1. In a medium bowl, sift and whisk 2 cups flour, 1 teaspoon baking soda

    2. Brownie Mix

  5. Prepare to Bake

  6. Enjoy!
